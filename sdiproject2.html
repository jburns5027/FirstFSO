My weight loss is a life change. I say this because I had to change how I think about food, how I think about exercise, and must never go back to my old way of thinking. It was never just going on a diet, because “diets” end, and staying fit, can never.

So my new life style begins minutes after my feet hit the floor in the morning. I take a Men’s one-A-day multi vitamin, each and everyday. There are a lot of vitamins per bottle so it does last a long time, but from now on, that is something I will replenish every time the last vitamin in that bottle is take.

When it comes to working, esp. for a beginner, I had to start of with the tools I had. Not having a lot of money to spend on exercise equipment, means I needed to improvise. Since my Fiancée already had Jillian Michael’s Last chance Workout DVD, and JM’s 6 week to six pack, I started there. The Last Chance workout DVD, I used for my upper body, 6 Week to 6 pack DVD was for my core, and I run for my lower body.

I am lucky to live a condo complex with Meredyth, because we have access to a tiny gym. A few free weights, a bench, one machine, and 3 treadmills, and a mirror wall straight out of the 70’s, is all that takes up the small room they call a gym. During the summer, I run outside, bike outside, and fallow along to Jillian’s DVD I our living room. I rarly go to the gym during the summer. During the cold winter months, I feel stuck in the gym, so when it’s not cold, I stay out.
